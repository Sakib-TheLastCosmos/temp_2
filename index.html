HSC exams in all division postponed till July 18

Inter-Education Board issues a notice in this regard on Tuesday

https://www.dhakatribune.com/349953CopyFacebookMessengerTwitterViberWhatsappLinkedinPinterestPrint
File image of an exam hall. Photo: Dhaka Tribune
Tribune Desk
Publish : 22 Jun 2024, 07:44 PMUpdate : 22 Jun 2024, 07:44 PM

The authorities have postponed the Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) and other equivalent examinations in Sylhet division until July 8 due to the ongoing flood.

The Inter-Education Board has issued a notice postponing the examination on Saturday.

The HSC examinations are scheduled to take place from 30 June across the country.

According to the notice, as the Sylhet region's flood situation has worsened, the HSC and equivalent examinations under the Sylhet Division's Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board, Bangladesh Madrasa Education Board and Bangladesh Technical Education Board will be suspended till July 8.

The exam will be held from July 9 as per the published routine.

  • HSC exams from June 30, coaching centres to be closed for 44 days

The schedule of postponed examinations will be notified later through notification.

The notification further informs that the exams under Bangladesh Madrasa Education Board, Bangladesh Technical Education Board and other eight (except Sylhet Education Board) general education board exams will be held from June 30 as per published schedule.
